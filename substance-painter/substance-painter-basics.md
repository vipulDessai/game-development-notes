# shortcuts

## viewport

- f1, f2, f3

## move, rotate, zoom

- rotate - alt + left click and move
- zoom - alt + right click and move
- move - ctrl + alt + left click and move

## adjust light

- shift + right click

## bake

- press b to cycle through maps
- press m for material map
