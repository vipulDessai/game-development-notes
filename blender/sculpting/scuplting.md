# Add

- start with mesh ico sphere, shift a
  - set property - subdivisions to 7

# zooming & pan

- shift + middle mouse for pan
- ctrl + middle mouse for zoom

# Strength & radius

- press F for radius & move mouse up down
  - tap to exit
- press shift + F + move mouse up down for strength
  - tap to exit
