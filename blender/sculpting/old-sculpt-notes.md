## Brush

- To adjust the brush press <kbd>F</kbd>
- To adjust the brush strenth press <kbd>F</kbd> + <kbd>F</kbd>

### Smooth brush

- hold <kbd>SHIFT</kbd> and tap

### Subtract brush

- hold <kbd>CTRL</kbd> and tap

### Texture
