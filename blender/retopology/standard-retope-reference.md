    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>E-Pole</strong></td>
      <td>5 edges</td>
      <td>Extrusions (e.g. mouth, eyes, limbs)</td>
      <td>Spreads edge flow outward</td>
      <td>Can flatten or distort nearby loops</td>
    </tr>

- use inset (press <kbd>i</kbd>)
